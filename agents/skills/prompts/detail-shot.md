# Detail Shot Prompt

Extreme close-up showing product build quality, materials, and craftsmanship. Clinical, technical photography.

## Template (Product Background)

For [[../dzine/product-background]]:

```
Pure white seamless studio surface. Soft even diffused lighting from
two large softboxes positioned above and to the sides. Minimal shadows,
bright and clean high-key environment. Professional e-commerce product
photography setup. Neutral, clinical, technical aesthetic.
Top-down 90-degree overhead perspective for maximum detail visibility.
```

## Template (Img2Img)

For [[../dzine/img2img-workflow]]:

```
Extreme macro close-up of {product_name} showing surface texture,
material quality, and mechanical details. Pure white seamless background.
Soft diffused studio softbox lighting from above, minimal shadows.
105mm macro lens, very shallow depth of field, razor-sharp focus on
primary detail area. Top-down 90-degree overhead angle.
Clinical technical photography, precision engineering showcase.
Product unmodified, accurate textures and details preserved.
```

## Key Visual Elements

- **Surface**: Pure white seamless (NOT dark, NOT textured)
- **Lighting**: Even, diffused, high-key (NOT dramatic)
- **Camera**: Top-down 90 degrees OR extreme macro angle
- **Focus**: Ultra-sharp on texture/detail, shallow DOF
- **Mood**: Clinical, technical, precision
- **Aspect**: 1:1 square (NOT 16:9)

## What Makes It Distinct

- vs [[hero-shot]]: White background vs dark. Even light vs dramatic.
- vs [[lifestyle-shot]]: No environment context. Isolated product detail.
- vs [[mood-shot]]: Bright and clinical vs dark and emotional.
- Only variant that uses 1:1 square aspect ratio.
