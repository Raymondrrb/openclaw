# QA Checklist (Before Publish)

1. All claims have sources.
2. Prices and ratings verified on publish day.
3. Disclosures included in description.
4. AI disclosure included in description and on-screen (if possible).
5. Audio is clear and normalized.
6. Captions are enabled and readable on mobile.
7. Visuals match the spoken claim.
8. Title and thumbnail are accurate and not misleading.
9. Affiliate links tested.
