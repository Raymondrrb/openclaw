## Editing Style Analysis

### Structure
1. **Hook** (0:00-0:15): Opening B-roll montage with title text overlay, fast cuts between multiple vacuums in action
2. **Product Segments** (0:15-7:30): Repeating pattern per product (~1.5 min each):
   - Product hero shot (full vacuum, stationary or in use)
   - Close-up feature footage (roller head, dustbin, buttons, handle)
   - Lifestyle usage footage (person vacuuming in real home settings)
   - Text overlays naming key features over action B-roll
   - Brand promo footage with manufacturer text/logo visible
3. **Closing** (7:30-8:04): Final summary shots, CTA for links in description

## Key Insights for Rayviews Pipeline

### 1. Faceless B-Roll Format Outperforms Talking-Head for This Niche
87K views and 9.5% like ratio vs. the smartwatch talking-head study (45K views, 5.7% like ratio). The faceless format with quality B-roll generates nearly 2x the views and significantly better engagement. **This validates our faceless-first approach.**

### 2. Manufacturer B-Roll is the Primary Content Source
Nearly all footage comes from manufacturer promotional videos — the kind available on brand YouTube channels, press kits, and product landing pages. For Rayviews, we need to replicate this quality tier using Dzine AI image generation and Minimax Hailuo 2.3 video generation. The key is **realistic product footage in real environments**, not studio white-background shots.

### 3. Text Overlays are Feature-Focused, Not Product-Focused
The text overlays name features ("tangle-resistant roller") not products ("Dyson V8 Plus"). This keeps the viewer focused on benefits rather than brands. **Rayviews should adopt feature-callout text overlays on B-roll — this is a natural fit for our DaVinci Resolve templates.**

### 4. Real-World Environments are Critical for Trust
All footage shows vacuums in actual homes — kitchens, living rooms, hardwood floors, carpets, under sofas. No sterile studio shots. This matches our 40+/50+ target audience who need to see products in contexts that look like their own homes. **Dzine prompts must specify realistic home environments.**

### 5. Higher Production Quality = Higher Engagement
Compared to the Product Guide study (vERSwbJb-rg), this video has sharper footage, better text overlay design, and more consistent visual flow. The result: significantly better engagement metrics. **Investing in image/video quality (4K Dzine, careful prompting) directly impacts performance.**

### 6. Short Duration (8 min) Works for 5 Products
At ~1.5 min per product, the video keeps each segment tight and information-dense. No filler, no extended comparisons. **Our script word count target (1300-1800 words at 155 WPM = 8.4-11.6 min) is well-calibrated for this format.**

### 7. Description Monetization Pattern
Fire emoji + general affiliate link, then numbered product list with checkmark emojis and individual amzn.to links, "What You'll Learn" section, hashtags. **This is a proven description template we should adopt directly.**
