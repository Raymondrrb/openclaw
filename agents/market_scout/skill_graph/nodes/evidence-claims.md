# Evidence and Claims

Every ranking claim should tie to observable evidence.

## Required per Product

- 3 practical benefits.
- 1 honest downside.
- Best-for profile and skip-for profile.
- Price note with volatility disclaimer.

Do not use absolute claims when evidence is comparative or partial.
