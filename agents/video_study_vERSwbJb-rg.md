## Key Insights for Rayviews Pipeline

### 1. This IS Our Format
Fully faceless, manufacturer B-roll, text overlays, affiliate links, TTS voiceover. This is not "similar" to Rayviews — it IS the format we are building. Every production decision maps 1:1 to our pipeline.

### 2. Manufacturer B-Roll = Dzine AI Output
All footage is professional product shots and lifestyle demos from brand promo materials. This is exactly what Dzine AI can generate: product clarity shots, usage demonstrations, lifestyle contexts, feature close-ups. No Amazon screenshots, no webcam footage, no screen recordings.

### 3. Text Overlay Style Should Become Our Template
The clean white sans-serif with decorative line elements is professional and readable. Key pattern: short feature name (2-5 words) positioned upper-left or center-left over action footage. This is trivially implementable in DaVinci Resolve as a reusable Fusion template.

### 4. Search-Bar Title Card is a Smart Hook
The search-bar-style intro (mimicking a Google search) is a clever trust signal — it implies "I searched so you don't have to." This is worth adapting for Rayviews with our own branded version.

### 5. Description Format is Clean and Effective
Numbered list with checkmark emojis, product name, "Check Prices" text, and short affiliate link. Amazon Associates disclosure at bottom. Both amzn.to and geni.us link formats used. This is the exact description template Rayviews should use.

### 6. No Talking Head Needed = Full Automation
Zero human appearance means the entire video can be automated end-to-end: research, script, Dzine images/video, ElevenLabs TTS, DaVinci Resolve assembly. No Lip Sync needed, no avatar consistency issues, no character management overhead.

### 7. Spec Overlays Drive Perceived Authority
Specific numbers in overlays ("450 Watt Motor," "15,000 Times per Second," "Retractable 30 Foot Cord") create trust through specificity. The research pipeline already extracts these specs — they should flow directly into overlay templates.

### 8. Fast Pacing Keeps Engagement Without Effort
3-5 second clips with 2-3 second text overlays means ~15-20 visual changes per minute. This pace is achievable with automated timeline assembly — just alternate between Dzine product images/clips and add text overlays at fixed intervals.
