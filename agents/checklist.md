# Checklist (Compliance)

YouTube:

- Video is original and adds value.
- No reused content from other creators.
- AI disclosure included in video when required.

Amazon Associates:

- Description includes: "As an Amazon Associate I earn from qualifying purchases."
- Affiliate link is clearly disclosed.

FTC (ads/endorsements):

- Clear disclosure near the link.
- No misleading claims.

Content:

- All claims based on real, verifiable specs.
- No fake reviews or ratings.
